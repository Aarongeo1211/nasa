This visualization presents simulated data for various climate parameters relevant to irrigation in Bengaluru. The data spans from 2019-10-08 to 2029-10-05, including both historical and projected values. Please note that this is simulated data and should not be used for actual planning or decision-making.

Precipitation (mm/day)

The current evapotranspiration rate is 2.66 mm/day, lower than the average of 3.22 mm/day. With a decreasing trend, this indicates decreasing water demand from crops and soil, which may affect irrigation scheduling and water resource management.
